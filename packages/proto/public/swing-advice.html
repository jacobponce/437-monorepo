Game Improvement - Swing Advice

Quick Tips

Keep Your Head Still

Focus on keeping your head in place through impact.

Proper Weight Transfer

Shift your weight from inside of the back foot to front foot heel during the downswing. This creates power and consistent ball striking.

Maintain Tempo

A smooth, consistent tempo is more important than swinging hard. Use an app or video to practice your tempo. Practice your tempo doing air swings ANYWHERE
